I'm Jonathan Alumbaugh,

a New England native with an education in photojournalism and graphic design. I'm interested in ways of promoting community through design. I hope to observe, tell the story of, and refine the interaction between people and design, and between people and people.

Someday I'll find myself halfway across the world (likely without a jacket or a clean pair of socks) in pursuit of a design challenge. In the meantime, I seeks a design challenge on the homefront, in the Rhode Island/Southcoast/Boston area.
